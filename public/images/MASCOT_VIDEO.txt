LEFT SWIPE VIDEO (taco)
The app picks one of these at random each time you swipe left:

  taco encouraging A.mp4
  taco encouraging B.mp4
  taco encouraging C.mp4
  taco encouraging D.mp4

Keep them in this folder (public/images). Poster + fallback: swipe-left-mascot.png

Optional: override with .env
  VITE_SWIPE_LEFT_MASCOT_VIDEO_URL=https://...
  VITE_SWIPE_LEFT_MASCOT_WEBM_URL=https://...

To add another clip, use the same naming pattern: taco encouraging E.mp4
Then add the filename to TACO_ENCOURAGEMENT_VIDEOS in src/components/VideoFeed.tsx
